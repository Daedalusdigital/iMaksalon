About iMakApp

WHY SHOULD YOU DOWNLOAD iMakApp?

iMakApp is a mobile beauty and stylist service that uses an application platform to connect the clients and iMakeApp beautician and stylist. An iMakApp beautician will provide the service of make-up, hair styling, manicure and pedicure in the comfort of their home. iMakApp is created to give a platform for professional women who do not have time to spend at the salon and prefer the service to come to them at the comfort of their homes, and give the beauticians a platform where they don’t need to go out looking for clients but clients are available on the platform..

Our Vision

We envision a chain of salons and stylists in different communities which are linked to through iMakApp
